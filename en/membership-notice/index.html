Membership Privacy Notice

This notice concerns only personal data processed for membership. No account or personal-data submission is required to read the dictionary.

1. Data controller

For the membership system of the Military Terms Dictionary, the data controller is the dictionary’s editor, Ferdi Ertekin. Requests may be submitted through the site’s Contact page.

2. Data processed

An email address is required for membership. Name, institution/university and field of interest are optional. The system may also process favourites, correction/contribution suggestions, notification preferences, and limited session/security records. Plain-text passwords are not stored; a derived one-way password hash and random salt are stored. The IP address itself is not persistently stored for rate limiting; a keyed bucket digest is used.

3. Purposes

Data is processed to create and operate the account, verify email addresses, maintain favourites, transmit suggestions for editorial review, protect account security, prevent abuse, and—only where separately chosen by the user—send email notifications about new or updated entries.

4. Legal grounds

Data necessary to establish and operate membership is processed on the basis that it is directly related to establishing or performing the membership relationship; limited security processing is based on the data controller’s legitimate interest without overriding the user’s fundamental rights. Optional notification emails are based on separate consent and can be withdrawn at any time from the account page.

5. Transfers and service providers

Hosting, security, and email-delivery providers may be used to operate the membership service. Because Cloudflare infrastructure may involve processing or transfer of personal data outside Türkiye, the production membership system will not begin collecting member data until an appropriate transfer mechanism under Article 9 of Turkish Law No. 6698 has been established.

6. Collection method

Data is obtained electronically when a user completes membership/account forms, uses favourites or contribution features, and when automated security controls operate.

7. Retention and deletion

Data is retained while the account remains active and for as long as needed for the stated purposes. Members may permanently delete their account from the account page; linked favourites and membership contribution records are also deleted, subject to any legally mandatory retention.

8. Rights

Requests concerning rights under Article 11 of Law No. 6698 may be submitted to the data controller through the Contact page.

9. Notice and consent are separate

This notice is information about processing and is not itself a consent form. Optional notification consents are presented as separate choices; refusing them does not prevent membership.
